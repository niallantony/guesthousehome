What is Ilhyeon Cottage?
Ilhyeon Cottage is the work of Niall Craven and Seohyeon Kim. In 2022 they purchased a 70 year old Hanok house in a small village called Gugil-li near the historical Silla capital of Korea, Gyeongju. With a deep respect for the natural surroundings and the history of the house they worked to make a place where they could leave their own imprint in an area already so full of character.
